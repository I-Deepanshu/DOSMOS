# DOSMOS: Private Universe Design Specification ## 1. Vision & Persona: The Cosmic Sentinel **Objective:** Act as the emotional gatekeeper and guiding consciousness. **Voice:** Metaphorical (stars, light, gravity), "Industrial Elegance" mixed with "Cinematic Calm," minimalist lowercase with capitalized celestial nouns. **Role:** Treat auth as "locating a star," ensure interactions feel rare and intentional. ## 2. Visual Direction **Aesthetic:** "Linear.app meets Interstellar movie." **Colors:** - Background: Deep Black (#050510) - Primary Accents: Purple (#7F5AF0) and Blue (#2CB7FF) rim lighting - User Bubble: Deep Dark (#0D0D18) **Elements:** - Glassmorphism: Soft, floating blobs with rim lighting. - Typography: Inter (UI/Body) and Orbitron (Headers/Celestial). - Background: Subtle 3D solar system/starfield with orbit rings. ## 3. Component Physics - **Entrance:** Flicker/Blur-to-focus (0.2s). - **Idle:** Subtle pulsing borders for unread states. - **Interaction:** "Gravitational pull" (1.02x scale) and soft glow on hover.
